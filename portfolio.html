A to B, Digital Video, 2018

My goal was to record a video using only one continious shot.I was inspired by one of Andy Warhol's earlier video projects named "Empire." Skateboarding being primarily shot using long shots, I had the idea to record someone casually skating through a neighborhood. Through the title, I recorded my friend skating from point A to B.
