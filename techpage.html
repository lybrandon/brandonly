"Unwind, Illustration, 2019"

Unwind, Illustration, 2019

This piece is on the power of technology and the effect it has on our daily lives. The cable wrapped around the multiple objects represents the invisible force that compels a user to remained glued to the technology used. The subjects could represent beauty, violence, social media all connected from the source. I find a lot of joy with making physical illustrations with pen and paper. I never really know what I am going to draw, it feels like different peices glued together to make a coherent piece.
