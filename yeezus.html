Yeezus Christ, Digital Print, 2019

I made this Digital peice using Photoshop and photos sourced on the internet. This was in response to Kanye West visiting the White House. It made me think how much influence does somebody have for the whole world to have eyes on you when visiting the President. I have a lot of fun playing with satire and playing around with digital tools. You can make a lot of hilarous and ridiculous things with photoshop.
